03 · Products

Products & Services

We offer two categories of products and services:

  • Handicrafts: Authentic, handmade Indian artisan products including paintings, marble inlay work, wooden carvings, crochet items, Zardozi embroidery, and textile pouches. All items are handcrafted and may exhibit slight natural variations which are a mark of their authenticity.
  • Travel Packages: Curated cultural travel experiences to Agra, Delhi, Jaipur, and other heritage destinations. Packages may include accommodation, guided tours, transfers, and cultural activities as described on each package page.

We take great care to represent our products accurately. Colours may vary slightly due to photography and screen calibration differences. All product weights and dimensions are approximate.
